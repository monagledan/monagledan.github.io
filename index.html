PhD Research
Magnetic Energy Harvesting

Designing circuits that extract energy from the magnetic fields surrounding current-carrying conductors. My work spans nonlinear analytical modeling, resonant circuit topologies, and hybrid multi-core architectures — enabling self-powered sensors that can monitor electrical infrastructure without any batteries at all.
